About Us

We, Nandadeep Sales, got established in the year 2010, as a wholesaler, supplier and trader of Industrial Tools and Accessories. Our offered, industrial products such as Hand Tool, Power Tools and Industrial Bearing are highly acclaimed and appreciated by the clients. We have acquired expertise in the supply of silicone sealant, which is specially formulated for bonding and repairing of household appliances.

Our supplied silicone sealant is commonly used for sealing the air-conditioner ducts and water boats. Further, our traded, oil paints are specially formulated for painting the substrates. Our supplied oil paints are commonly used for corrosion resistance. Additionally, our wholesaled, wire wrapping tools are specially designed to wrap the wires. Our supplied wire wrapping tools are made from high grade raw materials and components for ensuring longer serviceability and durability.

Our offered, sander is a unique device for facilitating the sanding operations. Our supplied sander is highly acclaimed and appreciated by the clients, due to the optimal stroke rate and load speed. Further, the offered, industrial products are made from superior grade raw materials and components.

We are greatly indebted to our mentor Mr. Narendra Ghanwat, who has always inspired and motivated us to deliver the best, industrial products. Our entire workforce wishes to work under his leadership to reach new horizons in the national market.
